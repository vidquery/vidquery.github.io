A Multi-Camera Visual Question Answering Dataset for Surveillance Applications

Abstract

We present the first Multi-View Visual Question Answering (MVQA) dataset specifically designed for the security surveillance domain. By integrating four public datasets‚ÄîNextQA, AI-Challenge Re-identification Dataset, and USGun Mock Attack Dataset‚Äîwe have curated a comprehensive dataset that includes 800 security surveillance-related MVQA questions. This dataset not only provides visual question-answer pairs but also multi-camera topology connection matrices and annotations. We believe this dataset will significantly advance research in the field of security surveillance and MVQA.

Datasets

1. NextQA Surveillance Dataset

Description: We manually selected 36 fixed-camera videos from NextQA to emulate surveillance views by eliminating handheld perspective variability.
Annotations: 300 MVQA questions focused on surveillance-relevant visual content.

2. AI-Challenge Re-identification Dataset

Description: Based on re-id scenes involving movement across multiple cameras.
Selection and Annotations:

  • Settings: 10 different environments with varying overlap (S01‚ÄìS21).
  • Clips: 20-second segments from timestamps 9000‚Äì9600.
  • Annotations: 5 question categories‚ÄîCamera Coverage, Location, Movement Monitoring, Movement Prediction, and Counting.
  • Question Generation: GPT-4 generated candidate questions, manually filtered to 300 final questions.

3. US Gun Mock Attack Dataset

Description: Focuses on coordinated mock attack scenarios across three cameras.
Selection and Annotations: 5 video clips (~10 minutes total), 20 questions about behaviors and events.
